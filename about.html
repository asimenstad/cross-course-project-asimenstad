About RainyDays

RainyDays was founded in 1997 and since then, our vision has been to give outdoor enthusiasts quality jackets for every outdoor activity you can think of.

We make rain jackets in supreme quality, suitable for every weather type there is and affordable for all. In close collaboration with our test team of professional adventurers, we develop high-quality jackets.

Our goal is to ensure you are comfortable on every adventure you go on - so you can keep pushing the comfort zone.
